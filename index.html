I am delightedly to have this space carved out for myself on the World Wide Web. 

The very first memory I have of sending out text documents out was on one of the very first laptops that Apple made called the iBook, through the very same TextEdit app that I am writing on now.


Over the history of the year 2000 to 2020, I have come into contact with several people online whom I admire, analyzing, discussing, and simply talking about the subject matter they find so invigorating they share in abundance.

I can confidently say I have that too.


Owing to the geographical frontiers I grew up in laden with rich biodiversity, I understood the wealth that came from natural landscapes.

I devoted 4 years of my undergraduate studies to understand what it takes to protect naturally occurring sources of wealth for a country like forests, land, and water.


These resources are finite and are vulnerable to the calamitous changes of our climate. 


I believe that with increasing climate action, we can give justice to that unjustly devastated. 


Storytelling is foundational to climate justice and underlines the importance of climate action.


A climate communicator is paramount.


Apprehensive, jaded, but hopeful,

Alice in Vancouver
